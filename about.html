About Us

Empowering Northern Cape communities through education, food security, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

ROCS (Recognize Obstacles Create Solutions) is dedicated to improving the lives of individuals in the Northern Cape region through sustainable development projects focusing on education, food security, and community growth. We believe that empowered communities are the key to unlocking long-term success for future generations.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, ROCS was established to address the pressing social issues in Williston and the broader Northern Cape. What started as a small after-school tutoring initiative has grown into a multi-faceted organization with programs designed to uplift the community through education, sustainable food security, and infrastructure development.

Our Vision

We envision a Northern Cape where every community has access to quality education, sustainable food sources, and the tools needed for self-sufficiency and growth.
